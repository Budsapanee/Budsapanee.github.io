About Me

Since 2015, I have worked as a UX/UI designer. My backgrounds in interaction design and computer engineering allows me to work well with software engineers. Throughout my eight years in the UX/UI field, I have developed my hard skills. I'm capable of doing UX research, creating prototypes, and getting them ready for implementation. But even though I realize soft skills are just as vital, I would like to go the next step and become a lead designer.

I attend Massachusetts College of Art and Design to further my studies. Design Innovation (MDes) is my major. I'm developing my soft skills like leadership, public speaking, and team building since I think that will help me in my future work as a lead designer.
