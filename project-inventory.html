The Problem

This project began with a requirement to increase features to support our clients' expanding operations. The beginnings of our clients' businesses remain straightforward. Their demands are still met by the functions that are provided. However, when the firm grows, owners require more complex features to run their own operations.

My Role

My responsibility is to conduct user research by interviewing users in various personas and business kinds that use stock management features to operate their businesses, such as trading or product manufacturing.

Following that, I created a prototype to test with users who fit each persona. When I got feedback or remarks, I used them to improve my designThen, summarize the user workflow for each role, like salespeople, accountants, and warehouse workers..

Finally, create a prototype to test and finalize the concept in preparation for implementation.

Challenge

This functionality is shared by many business users, personas, and roles. As a result, the ultimate problem is to organize visualization so that people may collaborate effectively, often known as "information architecture."

Furthermore, I must ensure consistency of use in software.
